The Problem

Why Surface Maintenance Matters for Commercial Properties

Commercial property appearance directly impacts lease rates and tenant retention. A building with peeling paint, faded striping, and rusted fixtures signals deferred maintenance — and prospective tenants notice. The cost of proper surface preparation and recoating is a fraction of the revenue lost from vacant units or below-market lease rates caused by poor property appearance.

Georgia's climate makes the problem worse. Summer heat and UV break down paint and coatings faster than in northern climates. Humidity drives rust on every exposed metal surface — railings, bollards, light poles, dumpster enclosures, stairways. Concrete sidewalks and parking areas stain from tire marks, oil, and organic growth. Without regular surface maintenance, commercial properties age visually much faster than their structural condition warrants.

Mobile blasting lets property managers address multiple surface issues in a single visit — building exterior, parking lot, site fixtures — without coordinating multiple specialized contractors. We handle the surface prep, and your painting or coating contractor handles the finish work on properly prepared surfaces.

FAQ

Property Management Blasting — Common Questions

Can you prep building exteriors for repainting?

Yes. We remove failing paint, staining, and surface contamination from metal siding, concrete block, brick, stucco, and tilt-up panels. Creates a clean, profiled surface for new coatings to bond properly.

Do you remove parking lot striping?

Yes. We remove old markings, handicap designations, fire lanes, and directional arrows from asphalt and concrete before re-striping — completely, without damaging the pavement.

Can you work around tenants?

Yes. We schedule around business hours and traffic patterns. Work can be phased by section, building face, or parking zone to minimize tenant and customer disruption.

What commercial surfaces can you blast?

Metal siding, concrete block, brick, stucco, tilt-up panels, railings, bollards, dumpster enclosures, light pole bases, concrete sidewalks, parking structures, and loading docks. We adjust media and pressure for each surface.
