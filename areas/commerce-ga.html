Why Commerce Properties Need Surface Preparation

The SK Battery America facility changed Commerce's industrial trajectory. The massive EV battery manufacturing plant near I-85 has drawn suppliers, logistics companies, and support businesses into the area, creating a cluster of industrial operations that all depend on maintained infrastructure. The Ridgeway 85 Logistics Center offers 315,000 square feet with 70-plus dock doors. Commerce Crossroads Building 400 provides 172,000 square feet of Class A space with 32-foot clear ceilings and 50-plus dock doors. More facilities are under development on 200-plus acres directly across I-85 from SK Battery.

All of this industrial space needs surface preparation — from day-one concrete floor profiling in new builds to ongoing rust remediation and coating maintenance as facilities age. Warehouse floors take abuse from forklifts and heavy loads. Steel structures and dock hardware corrode in Georgia's heat and humidity. Loading zones and parking areas need periodic striping removal and re-marking.

The retail side of Commerce's economy — anchored by Tanger Outlets with 70-plus stores and the Banks Crossing commercial area — adds demand for parking lot maintenance, building exterior prep, and commercial surface restoration. Our mobile setup handles both industrial and retail-scale projects efficiently, with no need to transport equipment or materials off-site.
