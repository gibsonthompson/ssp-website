Why Atlanta Properties Need Surface Preparation

Atlanta's industrial footprint is staggering. The Fulton Industrial corridor alone — running along Fulton Industrial Boulevard west of the city — is the largest industrial district in the eastern United States, with over 116 million square feet of warehouse and distribution space managed by its own Community Improvement District. The northeast corridor along I-85 through Gwinnett adds another 130 million square feet. The northwest I-75 corridor through Cobb has 34 million square feet. The south/airport corridor adds another 68 million square feet. All of this infrastructure needs maintained concrete, clean steel, functional coatings, and clear parking markings.

Beyond the industrial base, Atlanta's commercial and mixed-use development is among the most active in the country. Midtown's tower construction, Westside's adaptive reuse of industrial buildings into creative and commercial space, Buckhead's office and retail maintenance cycle, and the ongoing transformation of aging properties throughout the metro all create demand for professional surface preparation — coating removal before renovation, concrete profiling in new build-outs, rust remediation on exposed structural elements, and building exterior prep before repainting.

The construction industry itself is one of Atlanta's largest employers, and every piece of heavy equipment on every job site eventually needs cleaning, rust removal, or coating restoration. Our mobile blasting system handles it at the job site or equipment yard — no downtime hauling machines across the metro.
