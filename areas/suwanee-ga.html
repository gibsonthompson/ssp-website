Why Suwanee Properties Need Surface Preparation

Suwanee offers manufacturers a 100% Freeport tax exemption — one of the strongest incentives in metro Atlanta — which is why companies like Socomec, Mitsubishi Electric, and other advanced manufacturers have chosen to operate here. Every manufacturing and distribution facility depends on maintained concrete floors, structural steel, equipment, and building exteriors that face constant degradation from industrial use and Georgia's humid climate.

The commercial side of Suwanee is equally active. The Town Center district, Old Town Suwanee, and the Buford Highway corridor all contain retail, office, and mixed-use properties that need periodic surface maintenance — building exterior prep, parking lot striping removal, loading dock restoration, and rust remediation on outdoor fixtures. As Suwanee continues to densify with new developments like Town Center on Main, renovation and adaptive reuse projects add more demand for professional coating removal and surface preparation.
