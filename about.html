About Strickland Surface Preparation

Professional surface preparation built on reliability, quality, and honest communication.

Our Mission

At Strickland Surface Preparation, our mission is to provide reliable, high-quality surface preparation services, including coating removal and media blasting, for industrial and commercial clients throughout Georgia. We focus on mobile abrasive blasting to achieve optimal industrial surface preparation.

Based in Hall County, Georgia, we provide mobile abrasive blasting services across the state and throughout the Southeast. Our DB500 Dustless Blasting system delivers industrial-grade results directly at your job site.

We work with general contractors, property managers, manufacturing facilities, equipment dealers, and anyone who needs a surface stripped, cleaned, and prepped right. We use crushed glass as our primary media, with garnet, coal slag, soda, and specialty abrasives available for specific applications.
