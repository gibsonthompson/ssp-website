What We Do

Our Surface Preparation Services

From rust removal on structural steel to stripping coatings off heavy equipment, we handle every phase of surface preparation with industrial-grade mobile blasting systems.

Mobile Abrasive Blasting

On-site blasting using our DB500 mobile system with crushed glass media and specialty abrasives. We come to you — no transport needed.

Learn More

Rust Removal

Complete rust elimination from structural steel, equipment, railings, and metal surfaces. Prep for coating or full restoration.

Learn More

Paint & Coating Removal

Strip old paint, powder coating, epoxy, and protective coatings from equipment, buildings, and metal structures.

Learn More

Concrete Surface Preparation

Remove deep contaminants, laitance, and existing coatings from concrete floors and surfaces for new coating adhesion.

Learn More

Striping Removal

Remove old parking lot striping, road markings, and traffic paint from asphalt and concrete surfaces for clean re-striping.

Learn More

Heavy Equipment Cleaning

Restore construction equipment, agricultural machinery, and industrial assets to like-new condition.
