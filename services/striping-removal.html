Striping Removal

Old striping creates confusion and liability. We remove it for fresh markings.

Clean Slate for Your Parking Lot

Over time, parking lot striping accumulates layers of old paint. Eventually you can't paint over it — the buildup peels and old markings show through.

Our mobile system removes old striping, arrows, handicap stencils, fire lane markings, and curb paint from asphalt and concrete.

Applications

  • Re-Striping Prep — Remove old markings before fresh striping
  • Layout Changes — New traffic flow or ADA updates
  • Fire Lane & Handicap — Replace to meet current code
  • Warehouse Markings — Safety lines, forklift lanes

Who Uses This

  • Property management companies
  • Striping contractors needing prep
  • General contractors
  • Municipalities and DOT
  • Warehouse operators
