Mobile Abrasive Blasting

Industrial-grade mobile blasting that comes to you. No need to transport your equipment or assets — our DB500 system handles projects of any scale, anywhere in the Southeast.

On-Site Surface Preparation, Done Right

Our mobile abrasive blasting service eliminates the hassle and cost of transporting heavy equipment, structural steel, or vehicles to an off-site facility. We bring our professional DB500 Dustless Blasting system directly to your location — whether that's a construction site, warehouse, parking lot, or private property.

The DB500 combines water and abrasive media under high pressure, delivering powerful cleaning and surface profiling while suppressing dust. This makes it ideal for populated areas, commercial properties, and environmentally sensitive sites where traditional dry blasting would create issues.

Media Options for Every Surface

We select the right abrasive media based on your surface type, the coatings being removed, and the desired surface profile:

  • Crushed Glass — Our go-to media for most applications. Effective on steel, concrete, wood, and masonry. Produces a clean profile without embedding contaminants.
  • Garnet — Harder and faster-cutting than glass. Ideal for heavy rust and thick coatings on structural steel.
  • Coal Slag — Cost-effective for large-scale industrial projects where aggressive cutting is needed.
  • Soda (Baking Soda) — Gentle, non-destructive option for delicate surfaces, fire restoration, and applications where the substrate cannot be damaged.
  • Glass Beads — Used for peening and finishing applications where a smooth, polished surface is desired.

Applications

  • Structural steel preparation before coating
  • Equipment and machinery restoration
  • Building exterior cleaning and prep
  • Concrete floor and surface profiling
  • Vehicle and trailer paint stripping
  • Tank interior and exterior cleaning
  • Bridge and infrastructure maintenance
  • Graffiti removal from any surface

Why Mobile Matters

Transporting heavy equipment or large structural components to an off-site blast shop adds cost, time, and risk. With our mobile service, there are no transport fees, no logistical delays, and no risk of damage during transit. We set up on your site, execute the work, clean up, and leave your surface ready for the next phase.

This is especially valuable for construction sites where schedules are tight, industrial facilities that can't afford downtime, and property managers who need work done with minimal disruption to tenants and operations.
