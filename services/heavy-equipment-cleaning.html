Heavy Equipment Cleaning

We strip away years of rust, grease, and paint — restoring equipment ready for service or resale.

Restore Your Assets

Heavy equipment represents a major investment. Whether you're prepping for resale, refurbishing fleet assets, or cleaning after a lease return, proper surface preparation dramatically improves appearance and longevity.

We handle equipment of any size — small tools to full excavators, dozers, and trailers. We come to your yard, shop, or job site.

Equipment We Clean

  • Construction — Excavators, skid steers, backhoes, loaders, dozers
  • Agricultural — Tractors, combines, plows, planters, sprayers
  • Fleet Vehicles — Trucks, trailers, flatbeds, dump bodies, tankers
  • Industrial — Generators, compressors, conveyors, lifts, plant machinery
  • Rental Equipment — End-of-lease restoration and between-rental cleaning

For Dealers & Rental Companies

Clean equipment commands higher prices at auction and moves faster on your lot. We work with dealers and rental companies across Georgia for fast-turnaround mobile prep at your facility.
