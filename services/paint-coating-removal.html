Paint & Coating Removal

Old, failing coatings need to come off completely before new ones go on.

Strip Any Coating, Any Surface

Whether it's layers of old paint, failed epoxy, or flaking powder coat — our blasting removes it completely. No chemicals, no heat guns, no manual scraping.

Coatings We Remove

  • Latex & Oil-Based Paint — Single or multiple layers
  • Powder Coating — Factory finishes on equipment and parts
  • Epoxy & Urethane — Floor coatings, tank linings
  • Marine Coatings — Anti-fouling, bottom paint
  • Sealers — Silicone, acrylic, polyurethane

Why Blasting Beats Chemical Stripping

Chemical strippers are slow, messy, and create hazardous waste. Blasting removes coatings in a single pass while profiling the surface. Faster, cleaner, better result.
