Concrete Surface Preparation

Proper concrete prep is the difference between a coating that lasts 10 years and one that peels in 6 months.

The Foundation of Every Great Coating

Epoxy and polyurethane coatings are only as good as the surface beneath them. If concrete isn't properly prepared, the coating will fail.

What We Do

  • Coating Removal — Strip existing epoxy, paint, sealers
  • Laitance Removal — Remove weak cement paste
  • Contaminant Removal — Oil, grease, chemicals, embedded dirt
  • Surface Profiling — Create the proper CSP level
  • Stain Removal — Tire marks, rust stains, efflorescence

Who Needs This

  • Floor coating contractors
  • Property managers recoating parking decks
  • Manufacturing plants resurfacing production areas
  • GCs preparing new concrete for coatings
